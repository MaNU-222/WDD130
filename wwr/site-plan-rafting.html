Overview

Purpose

Our objective for this website is to urge all visitors that, they should choose our company over any other rafting companies because of our highly trained staffs and their enthusiasm for their jobs. Rafting is very competitive and risk taking, but enjoyable to be on the lake or river with our skilled men and women. People will have the opportunity to participate in a customer review section on the site, which is another reason for visitors to pick us. With the aid of the website, we will speed up and simplify your booking procedure. Even if you have not yet gone rafting, the site will offer you a sneak peek of the experience.

Audience

This website's target audience is individuals on vacation around the world, who want to have good time and enbark on an adventure. Customers will be between the ages of 15 and 50, and they will be safeguarded and given a life jacket. We will provide housing for our customers, such as hotels and tents. The majority of visitors will access the site via mobile device rather than desktop computer. For our potential customers, there will be social media outlets.
